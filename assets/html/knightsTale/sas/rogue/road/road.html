On the Road Again.

You decide to take the easiest path, and take a nice stroll down the road. Phillip and you are making great time when suddenly, 3 men jump out in front of you. Their faces are covered with red bandanas. "Give us all your coin and weapons! And don't you even think about fighting back! We're much too quick for the likes of you."

You look to Phillip, who is watching you, ready to follow your lead. What's your play?
