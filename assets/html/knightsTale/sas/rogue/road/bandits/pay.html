Pay the Men!

You quickly look down at your waist, reaching to pull out your coin purse to pay off these ruffians. As your fingers wrap around the soft supple leather purse, you see an axe suddenly embed itself in your chest.

The air is knocked from your lungs and you feel yourself falling. As you realize what is happening, the rogue stands over you, looking down. You hear him say, "An easy mark." as he begins to rifle through your pockets and you slip into blackness.

You Died.
