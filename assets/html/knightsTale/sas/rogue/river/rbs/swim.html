Time for a Swim.

Your companion politely declines to swim first, and allows you to take the lead. As you wade deeper into the water, the current begins to sweep you along, the weight of your armor continually pulling you under. You struggle for breath.

As you bob up one final time, taking in the deepest breath you can, you see Phillip, standing on the shoreline, his forehead resting in his hand as he shakes his head back and forth. He turns, and heads back for the castle.

You Died.
