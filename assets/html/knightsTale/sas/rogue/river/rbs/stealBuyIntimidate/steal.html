Steal the Boat!

You boldly decide that the law does not apply when the Princess' life is in jeopardy. With Phillip's help, the two of you are able to steal the boat from the dock and begin quickly sailing away. However, before you get 20 yards from the dock, you hear a man's outraged cry as he sees you floating away in his boat. You hear him call for the guards as you slip around the first bend in the river.

After about an hour, you see the tower looming up in sight. Phillip instructs you to pull the boat off the river and to approach the rest of the way on foot. As you finish pulling the boat up, you hear hoofbeats coming towards you. Turning the corner of the road are 5 mounted guards. The sieze you and take you into custody.

You and Phillip are taken back to the city, where you are hanged for being thieves.

You Died.
