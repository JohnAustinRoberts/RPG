To the River!

You decide to be cheap about it, and build your own raft. Phillip helps you gather up the supplies, and the two of you quickly fashion together a makeshift raft. Without much further ado, you and Phillip climb aboard and send yourselves down the river.

All goes well for the most part, until a tree branch falls from an overhanging elm, landing on Phillip, in the center of the raft. The impact knocks Phillip unconcious, breaks the raft apart, and the weight of the limb drags Phillip to the bottom. You barely make it to the river's edge, and looking back, realize Phillip has drowned. You continue following the river, and after a few hard hours of trekking, you find a stone tower erected on an island in the center of the river, a raised drawbridge connects the tower to the road passing by on the opposite side of the river.
