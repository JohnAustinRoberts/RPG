To the River!

You wisely decide that a boat would be the best option. You wouldn't have to waste time making a raft, and it's bound to be more sturdy. Phillip lets you know that there are a few ways to go about obtaining a boat for this journey.

You could just steal the boat, this would be the most straightforward option. You could attempt to buy the boat, but the owner may not be willing to sell. You could try to intimidate the boat owner into giving you his boat.
