The Rogue

You decide to choose the rogue. His name is Phillip Lightfingers. He stands at 5'3, and has a very plain looking face. He wears plain clothes aside from his leather jerkin. He is armed with daggers, a crossbow, and a map of the tower where the princess is being held.

Phillip lays out three possible routes to the tower, it is your choice as to which way you'll take, though he highly recommends taking the road.
