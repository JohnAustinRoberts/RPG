Sword and Board

You take up your bow and quiver, and head to the King's throne-room. Here, King Bogart needs your assistance. His daughter has been kidnapped by crazed fanatics of a nearby dragon cult. King Bogart tells you that if you are able to return with the princess, you will be paid handsomely, with rewards of gold, silver, and wine.

You need not go alone on this venture, I have a few squires here, all looking to make their names famous. Choose one to assist you on your quest.
