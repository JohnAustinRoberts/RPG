A Polite Refusal

You politely tell the man at the door that your days of adventuring are long behind you, and that due to your advanced age, you are unable to help. He looks at you sadly, and turns and walks away. You hear him mutter as he walks away, "Thie king will surely look down upon this."

As you lie down for bed that night, your front door is kicked in, and 6 men pour in, all wielding swords and daggers. You stand no chance, and are quickly overwhelmed. They stab you repeatedly, while almost chanting with each thrust, "In the name of the King. In the name of the King."

You Died.
